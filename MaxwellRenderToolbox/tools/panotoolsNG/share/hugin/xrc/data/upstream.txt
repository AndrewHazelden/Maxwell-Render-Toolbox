Hugin's source package also contains the following
(slightly modified) libraries. The improvements have been contributed
back to the upstream developers, if possible. Hugin would not have been
possible without them. Many thanks.

levmar by Manolis Lourakis http://www.ics.forth.gr/~lourakis/levmar
FLANN by Marius Muja and David G. Lowe, http://www.cs.ubc.ca/~mariusm/index.php/FLANN/FLANN (used unmodified)

The camera response estimation uses the EMoR response model developed 
at the Computer Vision Laboratory of the Columbia University. 
"Modeling the Space of Camera Response Functions," 
M.D. Grossberg and S.K. Nayar, 
IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence, 
Vol.26, No.10, pp.1272-1282, Oct, 2004.
